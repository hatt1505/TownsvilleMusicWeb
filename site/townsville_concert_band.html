THE TOWNSVILLE CONCERT BAND

The Local Community Band

This band meet once a week for rehearsals, and who play gigs around town throughout the year.

The band plays a range of different styles of concert band music, ranging from classical to musical theatre and modern.

All levels and ages are welcome.

The Townsville Concert Band are currently looking for new musician members.

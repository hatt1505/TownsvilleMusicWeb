Mission - Arushi

The Townsville Community Music Centre website is intended to recruit new members to organise, promote concerts and workshops as well as engage in volunteering to increase ticket sales and encourage performers to cooperate with promotion, photoshoots and media releases.

Success Evaluation - Arushi

The newly designed website will be made public for one month so that the increase in number of members would only be attributed to the refurbished website design .
At the end of every month, the number of new members and tickets sold will be counted and compared to the previous month's results.

Target Audience - Arushi

TCMC website "specialize in diversity" and as the theme changes for each concert, so the target audience changes as according to the theme.
This will suit the preferences for each concerts's target audience.
